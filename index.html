Our story

Born of a long northern winter.

What started as one Pastel ball python in a college dorm has grown into a small, intentional breeding program. We keep our numbers low so each snake gets individual attention, a clean environment, and time to grow out healthy.

We share everything we learn — diet, heating, handling — and we're always happy to answer questions, whether or not you're buying.
