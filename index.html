About Borealis Reptiles

Welcome to Borealis Reptiles, where passion meets expertise in snake breeding. We've been dedicated to producing healthy, beautiful snakes for over 10 years.

Our facility is designed with the welfare of our animals as the top priority. We maintain strict breeding standards and provide comprehensive care for all our snakes.

Our Mission

To provide reptile enthusiasts with healthy, well-socialized snakes while promoting responsible breeding practices and education.

Care Sheets

Ball Python Care

  • Temperature: 75-80°F ambient, 88-92°F basking
  • Humidity: 50-60%
  • Feeding: Weekly for juveniles, bi-weekly for adults
  • Enclosure: 40-gallon minimum for adults

Corn Snake Care

  • Temperature: 75-85°F ambient, 85-90°F basking
  • Humidity: 40-50%
  • Feeding: Weekly for juveniles, bi-weekly for adults
  • Enclosure: 20-gallon minimum for adults

King Snake Care

  • Temperature: 75-85°F ambient, 85-90°F basking
  • Humidity: 40-60%
  • Feeding: Weekly for juveniles, bi-weekly for adults
  • Enclosure: 30-gallon minimum for adults
